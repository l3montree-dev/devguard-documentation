# Code of Conduct

<InfoNotice message="Read DevGuard's full code of conduct" details="Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct" detailsLink="https://github.com/l3montree-dev/devguard/blob/main/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md"/>

At DevGuard, we are dedicated to creating a community that is free from harassment and inclusive for everyone, regardless of their background or identity. We expect all members to contribute to an open, welcoming, and respectful environment.

## Key Points
- Respect and Empathy: Treat everyone with kindness and consideration. Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Constructive Feedback: Provide and accept feedback gracefully. Learn from mistakes and focus on community growth.
- Professional Behavior: Avoid inappropriate language, harassment, and any unprofessional conduct.

## Enforcement
Community leaders will enforce these standards and take appropriate action against any unacceptable behavior. This includes removing harmful content and, if necessary, banning individuals who violate the code.

## Reporting
If you experience or witness any misconduct, please report it to community@devguard.org. All reports will be handled promptly and confidentially.
