The Explanation section provides comprehensive understanding of DevGuard's concepts, architecture, and methodologies. Unlike tutorials (which teach you how) or how-to guides (which solve specific problems), explanations clarify *why* things work the way they do and the reasoning behind design decisions.

This section covers the fundamental concepts, architectural decisions, and theoretical foundations that make DevGuard work. Each topic explores underlying principles, trade-offs, and contextual understanding needed to use DevGuard effectively.
